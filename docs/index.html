Alien P.R.O.B.E

an Android App for communication with Alien RFID readers developed for WKU PTS

About

This app was developed for the WKU Parking and Transportation services for the purpose of use in their daily activites. This android app works in conjunction with an Alien ALR-F800 reader and antenna to scan WKU parking tags. These parking tags are checked against the PTS database to enforce parking restrictions.
